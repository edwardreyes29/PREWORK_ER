Cat Mouse Game

By Edward Reyes

Rules:

1. Users can move the cat left and right with the arrow keys.

2. Users can press the spacebar to shoot a projectile.

3. Mice fall from the sky at random locations.

4. If a mouse reaches the bottom, the player loses a life.

5. If the player shoots a mouse, the mouse is deleted, and the player gets a point.

6. Once a player reaches 0 life, they lose.
